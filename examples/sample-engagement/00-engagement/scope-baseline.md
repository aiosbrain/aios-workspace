# Scope Baseline — Northwind Robotics

> **Synthetic example.** Northwind Robotics is a fictional company. All names, data,
> and decisions in this engagement are invented to exercise the Agentic Team Ops
> harnesses. Any resemblance to a real engagement is coincidental.

AI transformation engagement, Sprint 1 ("Foundations"). The contracted scope for the
engagement is the four deliverable tracks below. Anything not traceable to a track
here or to an accepted entry in `scope-ledger.md` is out of scope.

## Contracted deliverable tracks

### 1. AI Readiness Assessment
- 1.1 Organisation-wide readiness survey + scoring.
- 1.2 Findings report with prioritised recommendations.

### 2. Learning Journeys
- 2.1 Operations team learning journey (curriculum + facilitation).
- 2.2 Support team learning journey (curriculum + facilitation).

### 3. Governance Framework
- 3.1 AI usage policy + risk register.
- 3.2 Governance-as-code review gates (advisory).

### 4. Transformation Roadmap
- 4.1 12-month phased roadmap.
- 4.2 Next-steps recommendation / phase-2 proposal.

## Out of scope (unless a signed change adds it)
Net-new software builds, production systems, data-pipeline engineering, and
individual bespoke tooling are not part of Sprint 1 Foundations.
