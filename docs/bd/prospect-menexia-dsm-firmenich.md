## The Notco framing (use for Patrick)

Menexia raised **Notco (The Not Company)** as a competitive reference — an AI-native food product development company that has been building integrated AI for 10+ years and is now licensing their platform (Giuseppe / Concept Quant) to food brands including Kraft Heinz, Mars, Nestlé, Mondelēz. DSM-Firmenich's key customers.

**The distinction to use:**
Notco solves one function: formulation. It's a powerful specialist tool — but it doesn't connect to what came before (consumer insights, concept formation) or after (manufacturing, cost modelling, regulatory). It's a powerful node with no connective tissue.

AIOS is the connective tissue. It makes specialist tools like Giuseppe compound inside an organization rather than sit in another silo. If DSM-Firmenich were to adopt Notco's platform or build something like it, AIOS is what makes that investment actually pay off.

**Frame for Patrick:** "How have we been doing this without something like AIOS?"

Don't frame it as "AIOS vs. Notco." Frame it as: specialist AI tools proliferate, but without an organizational intelligence layer, each one is another silo. AIOS is the layer that makes all of it connect — including anything they've already invested in.
